About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at KAIST, South Korea, working in the Intelligent Design & Manufacturing Lab under Prof. Seunghwa Ryu. My research focuses on physics-informed machine learning — embedding governing physical laws directly into learning frameworks to enable data-efficient inverse design and material characterization in computational solid mechanics, especially in settings with sparse or noisy measurements.

I received my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Inha University and my M.S. from KAIST. This page collects my research, publications, and conference activity, and will keep growing as new work comes out.
